> This guide is split into easy step-by-step sections for a smooth and successful setup.

<Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase & Setup">
    ## 1️⃣ After Purchase

    Once you've completed your payment, you will receive:
    - A **license key** to activate the product  
    - A **download link** for the cheat loader  

    ## 2️⃣ Prepare Your System

    Before launching the loader, make sure your system is ready:

    - Disable all antivirus software and **Windows Defender**  
    - These security tools can block the cheat from launching or functioning properly  

    ## 3️⃣ Run the Loader

    Once the file is downloaded:

    - Right-click the file and choose **Run as administrator**  
    - Ensure antivirus protection remains disabled during this process  
    - On first launch, the process may take **2–7 minutes** — be patient  

    ## 4️⃣ Activate Your Key

    After the loader opens:

    - Click the **"Activation key"** button  
    - Enter the license key you received after purchase  

    ## 5️⃣ Select the Game & Load

    - Make sure the game is completely **closed**  
    - Select your game from the list  
    - Click **Load**  

    ## 6️⃣ Start the Game & Use the Cheat

    - When prompted, launch the game  
    - The cheat will initialize automatically  

    - Press **Insert** to open/close the menu at any time  

  </Step>
</Steps>
