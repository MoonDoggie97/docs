### Troubleshooting

Below is a variety of common errors. Expand the sections below to find the appropriate fixes.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Driver Initialization Failed">
    If you encounter this error, repeat the Windows Defender step from our [Pre-Setup Requirements](#pre-setup-instructions).

    Restart your PC after completing these steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cannot Connect to Server">
    Use a VPN such as [**WARP**](https://one.one.one.one/) and retry launching the loader.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Game Closing after Launch (Clicking INS in-game)">
    1. Run **Command Prompt** as Administrator.
    2. Paste the following command:

    ```bash
    bcdedit /set hypervisorlaunchtype off
    ```

    3. Restart your PC and try again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Other Issues">
    If you are still facing issues, you either didn't follow our instructions correctly, please carefully double check everything and retry.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
